Selene Workout:-

Anyone who has played Twister at a party knows that it is way harder than it looks on the cover of the box it comes in. The da Vinci workout takes the same principle one stage further making you work your muscles hard to help you get where you want to. 

Instructions: Mark 8 spots on the floor - you can use household objects, lie down on your back in the middle. Reach and tap the mark in the given order. Perform a bridge in between each try.

What it works: Hip flexors, glutes, lower back, obliques, upper abs, hamstrings, cardiovascular system. 

Make it better:  Nope, you can't. 

Make it harder: It's as hard as it needs to be already. 

Body types this can work for: Everyone who needs to challenge their agility and dexterity will find this useful. 

Perfect for: Developing greater muscle stability and stretching muscle groups that normally would not get to stretch a lot.
