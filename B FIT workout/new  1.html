Biceps, Glutes & Quads Workout:-

There are some muscle groups in the body that really have a significant impact on its performance of just about anything. Biceps, gluts and quads are key. Not only do they help you lift, carry, jump, walk and run better but they also become dynamos energizing the rest of the muscle groups in the body.

Instructions:Print the poster and cross out sets on completion. Complete all sets by the end of the day.

What it works: Biceps, quads and glutes, like advertised.

Make it better: When performing curls go on the balls of your feet challenging your core and abs.

Make it harder: Use heavier weights, if you have them around.

Body types this can work for: Anyone with biceps, quads and glutes (and that's all of us) can benefit from this workout.

Perfect for: Those looking to transform their bodies through a workout that piles on the pressure for the body to adapt.
