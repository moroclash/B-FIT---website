Combo Fighter Workout:-

When it comes to combination moves you know that you need speed, dexterity, agility, flexibility, power, aerobic endurance and a head that's firmly screwed onto your shoulders guiding it all. our Combo Fighter workout will push all your boundaries and then some.  

Instructions: Repeat each move with no rest in between until the set is done, rest up to 2 minutes and repeat the whole set again 3, 5 or 7 times depending on your fitness level.

What it works: Triceps, chest, deltoids, abs, quads, front hip flexor, side hip flexor, hamstrings, lower back, glutes, adductors, abductors, lower abs, cardiovascular system, aerobic performance (VO2 Max). 

Make it better:  Perform punching and kicking combinations on the balls of your feet so that you can swing your body's weight behind your punches. 

Make it harder: reduce rest time between sets to 60 seconds and seriously challenge your aerobic performance. 

Body types this can work for: Like most of our workouts this can work for practically anybody. Mesomorphs with their heavier muscles might find it a little harder but there is not much to it. 

Perfect for: Those looking to engage with their rivals in the Octagon. Plus it's great for anyone seeking to challenge their athletic performance by putting their body through a tough workout.
