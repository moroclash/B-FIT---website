Black Panther Workout:-

Power, strength, agility and a lithe physique are the Black Panther characteristics. The Black Panther workout is going to help you bring out the big cat hiding inside you. 

Instructions: Repeat each move with no rest in between until the set is done, rest up to 2 minutes and repeat the whole set again 3, 5 or 7 times depending on your fitness level.

What it works: Triceps. shoulders, abs, lower back, front hip flexors, lower abs, core, adductors, obliques, side hip flexors, hamstrings, glutes, upper back, biceps, cardiovascular system. 

Make it better: When performing body saw work pull your lower abs in towards your spine, flattening the muscles and forcing your core to work harder.   

Make it harder: Bring your knees up towards your waist when you do pull ups to challenge your abs and work your front hip flexors.

Body types this can work for: This is the perfect workout for any body type looking to develop strength, muscle control and agility. 

Perfect for: Feline types on the prowl for prey. Also great for anyone wanting to test and further develop the control they exert over their muscles.
